Checklist based testing involves predefined checkists or criteria to guide and structure of the testing process.

Review.

Testers refer to the checklist items to design test cases that target the listed items. By aligning their test cases with the checklist, testers can systematically examine and validate the desired behaviour, compliance, and quality attrivutes of the syten.

Promotes standardization and consistency in testing activities.
