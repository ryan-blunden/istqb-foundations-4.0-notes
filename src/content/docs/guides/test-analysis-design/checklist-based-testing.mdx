Checklist based testing involves predefined checklists or criteria to guide and structure of the testing process.

Typically based on industry best practices, internal pre-defined processes and standards, and domain or regulatory requirements.

By designing and aligning test cases with the checklists, testers can systematically examine and validate the desired behavior, compliance, and quality attributes of the system.

Promotes standardization and consistency in testing activities, regardless of level of experience.

Ensures important aspects are not overlooked.
